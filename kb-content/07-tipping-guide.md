# Tipping Guide — Norwegian Luna (Haven Suite)

## Overview

Tipping on a Haven sailing has two layers: the **pre-paid Daily Service Charge (DSC)** that NCL adds to your account, and **additional discretionary cash tips** expected for Haven-level service staff.

---

## Layer 1: Pre-Paid Daily Service Charge (DSC)

Your booking includes prepaid service charges for suites and The Haven (confirmed on reservation #64411979). This is a flat per-person, per-day fee pooled and distributed to dining room servers, galley staff, housekeeping, and other ship hotel staff.

### What DSC Covers
- Main dining room servers and assistants
- Buffet and food hall staff
- Stateroom steward (basic service)
- Behind-the-scenes galley staff, laundry, hotel operations

### What DSC Does NOT Cover
- Your personal Haven butler
- Haven concierge
- Specialty restaurant servers (beyond base DSC share)
- Spa technicians
- Bar staff (beyond service charge on bar bills)
- Shore excursion guides

---

## Layer 2: Haven-Specific Cash Tips

These are discretionary but genuinely expected by Haven staff. Cash tips are a meaningful part of their income. Strategic early tipping directly improves service.

### Recommended Amounts

| Role | Recommended Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Butler** | $150–300 total for 7 nights | $20–30/day for butler; more for extensive use |
| **Haven Concierge** | $50–100 total per couple | More if they go above and beyond |
| **Suite Steward** | $20–40 extra total | On top of DSC |
| **Haven Restaurant Maître d'** | $20–30 | If they managed GF needs attentively |
| **Specialty Restaurant Server** | $5–10 per person per meal | On top of gratuity already on bill |
| **Bar Staff** | $1–2 per drink | On top of 18–20% service charge |
| **Spa Technicians** | 15–20% of service cost | May be auto-added — check receipt. Holly's facial has 20% service fee already included. |
| **Pool Attendant (Haven)** | $10–20 for the week | Small tip, big goodwill |

### Butler Tipping Strategy

The butler tip is the most important:

- **Day 1 or Day 2:** Give $50–75 in cash, with a genuine "thank you for helping us get settled." Signals generosity and sets the tone.
- **Mid-cruise (Day 4):** Optional $20–30 if service has been outstanding.
- **Final evening (April 10):** Deliver the remaining bulk ($75–150) with a handwritten note. The note is optional but genuinely meaningful to staff working months far from home.

**Total butler tip target:** $150–200 for moderate use, $200–300 for heavy use.

---

## When to Tip

### Throughout the Cruise
- Bar tips: $1–2 cash per drink delivered
- Room service: $2–3 per delivery

### Day 1–2: Relationship-Setting Tips
- Butler initial tip
- Suite steward: $10–20 early sets the tone

### Final Evening (Friday, April 10)
Primary tip-giving moment:
- Butler: bulk of tip in a labeled envelope
- Concierge: their envelope
- Steward: their envelope

**Why final evening, not disembarkation morning?** Ensures staff can use it immediately. You won't be rushed during disembarkation.

### At Specialty Restaurants
- Service charges (18–20%) are often added to the bill — check before adding extra
- If server managed Holly's GF needs attentively, add $5–10 per person on top

---

## Cash vs. Onboard Account

**Cash is preferred for personal service tips** (butler, concierge, steward):
- Goes directly to the individual without pooling
- Personal and direct — recipient knows who it came from
- Traditional and universally understood

**Account tips** for dining and bar: adding to the bill is fine and standard.

### Cash to Bring

| Purpose | Budget |
|---|---|
| Butler | $200 |
| Concierge | $75 |
| Steward | $40 |
| Restaurant/Bar cash tips | $60 |
| Spa technicians (if additional treatments) | As needed |
| Port tips (guides, etc.) | $50 |
| **Total** | **~$425–500** |

Use $1, $5, $10, $20 bills. Pre-prepare labeled envelopes before the cruise.

## Tipping in Port Countries

### Belize (Harvest Caye — NCL Private Island)
- NCL-operated — tip as NCL staff: $1–2/drink, $5–10 for excursion guides
- USD standard

### Honduras (Roatán)
- Restaurant servers: 10–15% of bill
- Taxi drivers: rounding up appreciated, not required
- Excursion guides: $5–10/person
- USD accepted everywhere

### Mexico (Costa Maya & Cozumel)
- Restaurant servers: 15% standard; many tourist restaurants auto-add "propina" — check before adding
- Taxi drivers: round up
- Tour guides: $5–10/person
- Market vendors: no tipping for purchases

---

## Tip Envelopes

Prepare envelopes before the cruise:
- "For [Butler name] — with sincere gratitude"
- "For [Concierge name] — thank you"
- "For [Steward name] — thank you"

Ask butler and steward names on Day 1 and write them on envelopes. The personal touch is noticed and appreciated.
