# Entertainment Guide — Norwegian Luna

## Production Shows

### Rocket Man — Elton John Tribute ⭐ BOOKED for April 5 (Anniversary)
- **Venue:** Main theater
- **Duration:** ~75 minutes
- **What:** Full production tribute to Elton John — "Tiny Dancer," "Crocodile Rock," "Your Song," "Bennie and the Jets," "Rocket Man," and more. Elaborate costumes, full band, choreography, spectacular lighting. One of NCL's signature shows.

**Haven Access:**
- Ask Haven concierge on Day 1 to arrange priority seating — they hold a reserved section (typically center-front or premium seats)
- Two performances usually available (early ~7pm, late ~9:30pm). For anniversary dinner first, the late show works well.
- Confirm exact showtime with concierge — show schedule not available pre-cruise.

**Tips:**
- Dress up for the show — it's the anniversary evening
- Arrive 15 minutes before showtime even with priority seating
- Cocktails available at the theater bar before the show and during intermission

### Lunatique — Cirque-Style Production
- **Venue:** Main theater or specialty venue
- **Duration:** ~60–75 minutes
- **What:** Norwegian Luna's signature cirque-style aerial and acrobatic show. Aerial performers, contortionists, acrobats, dancers, live music. Visually spectacular. Typically performed earlier in the cruise (Days 2–3).

**Haven Access:** Request priority seating through concierge on embarkation day — this show tends to sell out.

**Tips:**
- Center seats have the best view for aerial acts
- Often has pre-show interaction with performers — arrive early
- Typically on a sea day or first/second port day evening

### HIKO — Magic & Illusion Show
- **Venue:** Main theater
- **Duration:** ~60–75 minutes
- **What:** Japanese-inspired magic and illusion show. Grand-scale illusions, close-up magic, theatrical storytelling. Combines traditional Japanese aesthetics with Las Vegas-style magic.

**Haven Access:** Priority seating available through concierge.

**Tips:**
- Front rows great for close-up magic elements
- Grand illusions best appreciated from mid-theater
- Runs once or twice during the cruise — don't miss it
- Sit closer if you want to be selected for audience participation

## Live Music Venues

### Syd Norman's Pour House — Rock Bar
- **Location:** Main ship
- **Hours:** Live music typically 9pm–midnight/1am
- **What:** NCL's signature rock bar. Live tribute bands nightly. Multiple themed nights: Eagles, Fleetwood Mac, classic rock anthems, 70s/80s.
- **Vibe:** High-energy, singalong-friendly. Dress casually. Average age skews 40+ — everyone knows the words.

**Notable Theme Nights:**
- Eagles Tribute — Hotel California, Take It Easy, Desperado
- Fleetwood Mac Tribute — Go Your Own Way, The Chain, Dreams
- Classic Rock Night — 60s–80s hits
- 80s Night — full dance floor

**Tips:**
- Gets crowded after 10pm — arrive early for a table or bar seat
- Standing room only common on Eagles/Fleetwood Mac nights
- Band takes requests — tip the bartender and ask
- No cover charge
- Drink packages apply
- Great for April 10 (last full evening)

### Haven Lounge — Private Bar
- **Location:** Haven complex, Deck 17/18
- **Hours:** Opens 11am; evening hours vary
- **What:** Exclusive Haven bar. Bar team learns your preferences by Day 2.
- **Use For:** Pre-dinner cocktails, late-night nightcaps, quiet relaxation

**Tips:**
- Tell bar team your drink preferences on Day 1
- Pre-dinner cocktails here before specialty restaurants is a great routine
- Late-night quiet spot after Syd Norman's energy
- Small plates and snacks often available during cocktail hour
- Drink packages typically include Haven bar — confirm with concierge

### Atrium Bar
- **Location:** Main ship atrium (center)
- **What:** Live music during the day and early evening — jazz, solo performers, acoustic sets. Transitions to DJ or trivia in evenings.

### Casino Bar
- **Location:** Casino floor
- **What:** Complimentary drinks for active players. Open as long as casino is running.

## Pool Deck Activities

- **Trivia** — Multiple sessions daily (music, general knowledge, pop culture). Team up with other guests.
- **Game Shows** — Family Feud-style, Newlywed Game, Price is Right parody.
- **Belly Flop Contest** — Mid-cruise sea day classic.
- **Live Music at Pool Deck** — Caribbean/reggae or pop duo, typically 11am–2pm.
- **Movie Under the Stars** — Movies on giant pool deck screen at night. Check Freestyle Daily.

## The Freestyle Daily

NCL's daily activity guide — lists every event, show time, dining hour, activity, and announcement for the following day.

- **Delivered to suite** the evening before (by 8–9pm)
- **Also available** digitally on the NCL app
- Review each evening to plan the next day
- Note booking requirements — some shows require advance reservations
- Check casino tournament times and special events

## Entertainment Tips

- Book show reservations the morning of or the day before — Haven concierge can hold Haven seats
- Syd Norman's doesn't require reservations — just show up
- Sea days fill up fast with activities — prioritize what matters
- Haven concierge knows the entertainment schedule in advance — ask on Day 1 for the full week overview
- Don't over-schedule — this is a vacation
