## Games

### Blackjack — Holly's Game
- Tables typically $10–25 minimum
- Best house edge of any table game with basic strategy (~0.5%)

**Basic Strategy Reminders:**
- Always: Split Aces and 8s
- Never: Split 10s or 5s
- Always: Stand on hard 17+
- Double down: on 11 (vs dealer 2–10), on 10 (vs dealer 2–9)
- Double down: on 9 (vs dealer 3–6)
- Stand: on hard 12–16 vs dealer 2–6 (let dealer bust)
- Hit: on hard 12–16 vs dealer 7–Ace
- **Never take insurance**

### Slots
- Wide variety — penny to $5+ machines
- Casinos at Sea points accrue from slot play
- Accessible 24 hours when in international waters

### Roulette
- American roulette standard (double zero)
- Better odds on European/single zero if available
- Even-money bets (red/black, odd/even) are casual-play friendly

### Poker
- Texas Hold'em typically available
- Check for poker tournaments — buy-ins usually $25–65

### Three Card Poker / Let It Ride
- Lower skill requirements, fun for casual play
- House edge higher than blackjack but rules are simple

## Casino Hours

- Casino opens once ship is in international waters — typically embarkation evening
