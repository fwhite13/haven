# Norwegian Luna — Ship Navigation Guide

## Ship Orientation
Norwegian Luna runs bow (forward) to stern (aft). Odd-numbered cabins port (left when facing bow), even starboard (right). Forward elevators near bow, aft elevators near stern. Mid-ship elevators amidships.

## Suite 12846 Location
Deck 12, starboard side, aft of midship. Nearest elevator: midship elevator bank.

## Key Deck Locations
- Deck 6: Syd Norman's Pour House (entertainment, midship)
- Deck 7: Casino (midship), main dining areas
- Deck 8: Onda by Scarpetta (midship), Los Lobos (midship)
- Deck 12: Your suite (12846), cabin decks
- Deck 15: Mandara Spa, Thermal Suite (forward), Main Pool (midship)
- Deck 17: Haven Restaurant (forward), Haven Lounge (forward), Haven Pool (forward)
- Deck 18: Haven Sundeck (forward), Haven outdoor areas

**To Main Pool (Deck 15, Midship):**

## Quick Reference
- Forward = toward bow (front of ship)
- Aft = toward stern (back of ship)
- Port = left side (odd cabin numbers)
- Starboard = right side (even cabin numbers)
- Your suite 12846 = Deck 12, starboard, midship-aft
