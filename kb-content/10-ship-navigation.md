# Norwegian Luna — Ship Navigation Guide

## Ship Orientation

- **Forward (Bow)** = front of ship
- **Aft (Stern)** = back of ship
- **Port** = left side when facing bow (odd cabin numbers)
- **Starboard** = right side when facing bow (even cabin numbers)

## Suite 12846 Location

- **Deck 12**, starboard side (even number = starboard)
- Nearest elevator: midship elevator bank
- The Haven complex is on Decks 17–18 (forward) — take the Haven elevator up

## Haven Keycard Access

Your Haven keycard (room key) is imprinted with Haven status and:
- Activates the Haven elevator — private elevator to Haven floors
- Unlocks the Haven pool gate and outdoor areas
- Identifies you as Haven to any staff on the ship — present it at any restaurant or venue for Haven-level service

## Getting Around Tips

- **Haven elevator** is the fastest vertical route — your keycard activates it
- The ship is large — midship elevators are closest to suite 12846
- Forward elevators serve the Haven complex
- On busy sea days, stairs between adjacent decks are faster than waiting for elevators
- The Freestyle Daily lists all venue locations — use it as a reference
