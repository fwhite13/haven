# Norwegian Luna — Ship Navigation Guide

## Ship Orientation

- **Forward (Bow)** = front of ship
- **Aft (Stern)** = back of ship
- **Port** = left side when facing bow (odd cabin numbers)
- **Starboard** = right side when facing bow (even cabin numbers)

## Suite 12846 Location

- Nearest elevator: **Haven Private Elevators (forward) — directly adjacent to suite 12846**
- Your Haven keycard activates the Haven elevator — use it for all vertical travel

## Getting Around Tips

- **Haven elevator is directly next to your suite** — it is always the fastest route. No midship walk needed.
- Your Haven keycard activates the Haven elevator — keep it accessible at all times
- The Haven complex is on **Deck 15** (not upper decks) — take the Haven elevator straight up
- For Deck 16 venues (spa, pool, slides), ride the Haven elevator to Deck 15 then take one flight of stairs
- For Deck 9 venues (restaurants, casino, bars), take the Haven elevator straight down
- On busy sea days, stairs between adjacent decks are faster than waiting for elevators
- The Freestyle Daily lists all venue locations — use it as a reference
